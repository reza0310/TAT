How do I access the map?

You can navigate to the map from the navigation bar and making your way to the home page. The map should appear on the right hand side of the page.

How do I book tickets?

You can book tickets once you have found a journey on the "Find Journeys" page which is located on the navigation bar.

Do I need to have an account to find journeys and track trains?

No, you do not need an account to use these features but you will require an account to archive journeys and store tickets on this website.
